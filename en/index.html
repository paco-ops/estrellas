ESTRELLAS
THE PLACE THE CLUB

Some places you visit.

Others you return to.

We live surrounded by noise.

Screens.
Notifications.
Constant speed.

And it’s becoming harder and harder
to find a place where you can truly switch off.
ESTRELLAS wasn’t created from an opportunity.

It came from a need:
for the world to slow down again.
It’s not a hotel.
It’s not a retreat.
It’s not a countryside escape.


It’s a place where the outside world fades away.
Where time begins to slow.
Where silence starts to matter again.
Two moments each year.

Always waiting for you.
It’s not a place you simply come to.

It’s a place you return to.
